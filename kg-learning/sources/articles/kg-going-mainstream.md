## Summary

Major organizations (SAP, Netflix, ServiceNow, Samsung) are adopting knowledge graphs as foundational infrastructure rather than optional technology. This represents a fundamental awakening to their strategic value.

## Three Core Drivers

1. **AI Requires Context**: Generative AI systems need structured, contextual foundations for accurate, explainable results

2. **Data Fragmentation Problem**: Organizations suffer from scattered schemas and duplicated logic. KGs unify meaning across the enterprise

3. **Semantic Accessibility**: Business users benefit from a semantic layer enabling natural question-answering

## Implementation Guidance

- Begin modestly with domain-specific applications
- Define core business concepts systematically
- Leverage existing data assets
- Maintain commitment to open standards
- Avoid treating graphs as universal solutions
